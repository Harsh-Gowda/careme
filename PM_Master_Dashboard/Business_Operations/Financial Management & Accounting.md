### Risk Register

| Risk | Likelihood | Impact | Mitigation | Owner |
|------|------------|--------|------------|-------|
| Envato account banned | Low | Critical | Diversify to own store | PM |
| Key developer leaves | Medium | High | Documentation, cross-training | CTO |
| Major security breach | Low | Critical | Security audit, insurance | Security |
| WordPress core changes | High | Medium | LTS versions, testing | Dev Lead |
| Competitor copies theme | High | Medium | Trademark, unique features | Legal |
| Payment processor freeze | Low | High | Multiple processors | Finance |
| Hosting outage | Medium | High | CDN, redundant hosting | DevOps |

### Business Continuity Plan

**Scenario: Envato Account Suspended**
1. Activate own store (WooCommerce/EDD) - 2 hours
2. Email all customers with new download link - 4 hours
3. Migrate support to own helpdesk - 1 day
4. Notify affiliates of new links - 1 day
5. Legal appeal to Envato - ongoing
