## Overview
We have developed three distinct design directions for the CareMe theme. Please review the options below and select your preferred style for the final implementation.

---

### Option 1: Standard (Modern Corporate)
The original CareMe aesthetic – clean, professional, and blue-tinted. Best for large medical groups.
- **Header:** Sticky, left-aligned logo, right-aligned nav.
- **Typography:** Sans-serif (Inter).
- **Primary Color:** Corporate Blue.

### Option 2: Minimalist (Centered)
A focused, modern layout with centered branding. Best for boutique clinics.
- **Header:** Centered logo, split navigation.
- **Typography:** Sans-serif (Inter).
- **Primary Color:** Corporate Blue.

### Option 3: Pearl Minimal (Premium Luxe)
Inspired by Pearl Dental – elegant, gold-accented, and high-contrast. Best for luxury dental practices.
- **Header:** Centered logo, contact info on left, CTA on right.
- **Typography:** Serif (Cormorant Garamond) for headings.
- **Primary Color:** Muted Gold (#D6C9AD).
